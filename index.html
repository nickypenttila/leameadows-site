    About Lea Meadows

    Lea Meadows believes the best stories are the ones you can curl up with, a pot of tea steeping nearby. Raised on Pride and Prejudice and the generous kindness of the JAFF community, she writes gentle, character-first fiction for readers who love a long walk, a witty letter, and a happy ending that feels earned.
