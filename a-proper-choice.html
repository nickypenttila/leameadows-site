A Proper Choice

A Pride and Prejudice Sequel

Coming September 21, 2026

Anne de Bourgh has survived Rosings once. Now she must decide whether she can return to it without becoming its prisoner again. Restored in health and newly certain of her own mind, Anne comes home to find that Lady Catherine still expects obedience, the county still expects an heiress, and even freedom requires practical arrangements: rooms, money, witnesses, a house with air in it, and a future chosen on purpose.

But Mr. Arthur Chelmsworth sees Anne not as an invalid to be managed, nor as a fortune to be claimed, but as a woman whose judgment deserves to stand. To choose him, Anne must first choose herself — publicly, materially, and in her own hand.
